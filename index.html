About Me

I am a multifaceted professional in the cannabis industry with expertise in sustainable systems, cultivation technology, and facilities management. With a passion for quality cannabis cultivation and breeding, I combine technical know-how with a deep appreciation for the plant.

Today, I bridge the gap between industrial expertise and cannabis cultivation knowledge, offering unique insights for businesses looking to optimize their operations.

My consulting services span facility design, compliance management, grow systems optimization, and genetics development. I'm committed to advancing the industry through innovation and sustainable practices.
